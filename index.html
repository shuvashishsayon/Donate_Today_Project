Syed Sayedul Haque Suman | Known as Barrister suman

Supreme Court lawyer Barrister Syed Saidul Haque Sumon has collected Tk 60 lakh in one day to help the flood victims of Sylhet-Sunamganj. Following the call on social media, various people from home and abroad sent the money to him through various channels.

Tasrif Khan | The Young Singer

Young singer Tashrif Khan raised a fund Rs 16 lakh for the flood victims in the early days of the floods in Sylhet. He later went live on Facebook to seek more help.

A total of over 2 crore BDT has been raised by young Tasrif. So far, more than 5,000 families have been given 12-15 day worth of food and medicines thanks to his efforts.

Tasrif, who is the vocalist of the band Kureghor, has hits like "Ami Mane Tumi" and "Bachelor" to his name.
